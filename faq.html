Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Kieseki and how it can help you start your business journey

What is Kieseki and how does it work?

Kieseki is a comprehensive digital business toolkit designed specifically for teenagers. It provides step-by-step guides, templates, and resources to help you start and grow your own business. The kit includes everything from business ideas to marketing strategies, all tailored for young entrepreneurs.

Is Kieseki suitable for complete beginners?

Absolutely! Kieseki is designed specifically for beginners. We provide clear, step-by-step instructions that guide you through every aspect of starting and running your business. No prior business knowledge is required.

How much time do I need to dedicate to my business?

Kieseki is designed to work around your school schedule. Most successful teen entrepreneurs dedicate 5-10 hours per week. Our flexible system allows you to work at your own pace, with many tasks taking just 15-30 minutes per day.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Yes, you can upgrade at any time! If you start with the Basic package and later want access to Premium features, you can upgrade by paying the difference. Contact our support team for assistance with upgrades.

How does the safety system work?

Kieseki includes a comprehensive safety system with parent notifications, secure meeting protocols, and financial safety features. All business activities are designed to be conducted with parental oversight, and our platform includes tools to ensure safe interactions with customers.

Do I need any special equipment or software?

You only need a smartphone or computer with internet access. All resources are digital and accessible through our platform. We recommend having access to a printer for some worksheets, but it's not required.

What if I need help or have questions?

We offer multiple support options: email support for all packages, priority support for Premium users, and an active community forum where you can connect with other teen entrepreneurs. Most questions are answered within 24 hours.
